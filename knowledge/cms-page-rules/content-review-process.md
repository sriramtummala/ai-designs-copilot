# Content Review and Approval Process
 
## Overview
 
All content generated or updated through the AI DesignOps Copilot must undergo a formal review and approval process before publishing.
 
## Workflow Steps
 
1.  **Draft Generation:** AI generates initial content draft based on user input.
2.  **Content Review (Marketing/Brand):** Review for brand voice, tone, messaging accuracy, and compliance with marketing guidelines.
3.  **Design Review (DesignOps/UI/UX):** Review for visual consistency, adherence to design system, and user experience.
4.  **Compliance Review (Legal/Accessibility):** Review for legal accuracy, accessibility standards, and regulatory compliance.
5.  **SEO Review (SEO Specialist):** Review for keyword optimization, meta data, and overall search engine friendliness.
6.  **Final Approval:** Head of Marketing/Product provides final sign-off.
7.  **Publishing:** Content is published to the CMS.
 
## Tools
 
*   Jira/GitHub Issues for tracking review tasks.
*   Collaboration platforms for feedback (e.g., Figma, Google Docs).
