# Legal Disclaimers Guidelines
 
## Overview
 
This document outlines the requirements for legal disclaimers on various content types, ensuring compliance with relevant regulations.
 
## Required Disclaimers
 
1.  **Investment Content:** Must include a disclaimer stating that content is for informational purposes only and not financial advice.
2.  **Health Content:** Must include a disclaimer that content is not medical advice and users should consult a healthcare professional.
3.  **Testimonials:** Must include a disclaimer that results may vary and are not guaranteed.
4.  **Affiliate Links:** Must clearly disclose the presence of affiliate links.
 
## Placement
 
*   **Website Footers:** General disclaimers.
*   **Specific Content:** Directly within or immediately below the relevant section.
 
## Language
 
*   Clear, unambiguous language.
*   Legally reviewed and approved wording.
