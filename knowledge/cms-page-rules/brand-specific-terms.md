# Brand-Specific Terminology Guidelines
 
## Overview
 
This document lists approved and prohibited terminology for our brand, ensuring consistent and accurate communication.
 
## Approved Terms
 
*   **AI Copilot:** Always use "AI Copilot" (two words, capitalized) when referring to our product.
*   **DesignOps:** Capitalize "DesignOps".
*   **Workflow Automation:** Use this phrase, not "process automation" or "task automation".
 
## Prohibited Terms
 
*   **AI Assistant:** Do not use, as it implies a different scope.
*   **Bot:** Avoid, as it can have negative connotations.
*   **Solution:** Use more specific terms like "platform," "tool," or "service."
 
## Usage
 
Refer to this list when creating any content to maintain brand consistency.
