# Blog Post Structure Guidelines
 
## Overview
 
This document defines the standard structure for all blog posts published on our platform, ensuring consistency and SEO best practices.
 
## Required Sections
 
1.  **Title (H1):** Clear, concise, and keyword-rich. Max 60 characters.
2.  **Author & Date:** Displayed prominently.
3.  **Introduction:** Briefly introduce the topic and hook the reader. (1-2 paragraphs)
4.  **Main Body (H2/H3):** Divided into logical sections with subheadings. Use bullet points and numbered lists for readability.
5.  **Conclusion:** Summarize key takeaways and provide a call to action. (1 paragraph)
6.  **Tags/Categories:** Relevant keywords for discoverability.
 
## Content Guidelines
 
*   **Word Count:** Aim for 800-1500 words.
*   **Readability:** Use short sentences and paragraphs. Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level target: 7-9.
*   **SEO:** Include primary keywords naturally throughout the text.
*   **Images:** At least one featured image, and relevant images within the body.
