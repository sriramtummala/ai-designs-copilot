# Image Licensing and Usage Policy
 
## Overview
 
This policy ensures that all images used in our content are properly licensed and adhere to copyright laws.
 
## Approved Sources
 
1.  **Stock Photo Libraries:** (e.g., Getty Images, Shutterstock) - Must have valid licenses.
2.  **Internal Asset Library:** Company-owned or created images.
3.  **Creative Commons:** Images with appropriate CC licenses, with attribution where required.
 
## Prohibited Sources
 
*   Random images from Google Image Search without verifying license.
*   Images from social media without explicit permission.
 
## Attribution
 
*   Provide clear attribution for Creative Commons and other licensed images as per their terms.
*   Store license information with the image asset.
 
 ## Consequences of Non-Compliance
 
Unauthorized use of images can lead to legal action and reputational damage.
