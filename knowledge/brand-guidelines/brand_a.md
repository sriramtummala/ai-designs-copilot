# Brand A Guidelines
 
## Tone of Voice
 
Brand A's tone of voice is **professional, innovative, and approachable**. Avoid overly casual language, slang, or aggressive marketing claims. Always maintain a positive and empowering stance.
 
## Prohibited Words
 
Do not use the word "revolutionary" or "groundbreaking" as they are overused and lack specific meaning. Instead, use "innovative" or "transformative."
 
## Required Elements
 
All blog posts must include a call to action (CTA) at the end.
