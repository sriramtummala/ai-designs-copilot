# Logo Usage Guidelines
 
## Clear Space
 
Maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the 'A' in the logotype. No other graphic elements should infringe upon this space.
 
## Minimum Size
 
For digital applications, the logo should not appear smaller than 32 pixels in height. For print, the minimum width is 1 inch.
 
## Color Variations
 
*   **Primary:** Full-color logo on light backgrounds.
*   **Reverse:** White logo on dark backgrounds.
*   **Monochrome:** Black logo on light backgrounds, or white logo on dark backgrounds for single-color applications.
 
## Misuse Cases
 
*   Do not stretch or distort the logo.
*   Do not change the colors of the logo.
*   Do not add effects (e.g., drop shadows, gradients).
*   Do not place the logo on busy backgrounds that compromise visibility.
